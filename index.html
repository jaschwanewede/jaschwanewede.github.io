About Me

Get to know me!

I'm Jack Schwanewede, a senior at Davidson College pursuing a B.S. in Cognitive Science with a Data Science minor. My academic and research work merges several disciplines, such as Computer Science and Psychology, to explore cognition and behavior in Virtual Reality.

I'm passionate about using technology to solve problems for the greater good, from analyzing how confounding variables can impact behavioral research to building datasets to investigate embedded inequities in video game statistics.

Beyond this, I love music, sports, gaming, and more. I’ve active in the Davidson music community since my freshman year, being the Principal Double Bassist in the Symphony Orchestra and playing bass guitar in several student bands. I help run the Davidson Games Club and love video games, especially RPGs, and am a big basketball fan.
