Network of Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Franchise Links

By Will Schuckenbrock and Jack Schwanewede

This visualization depicts crossovers between Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (2018) and several other video game franchises. Different video game franchises are represented by nodes (circles), and a crossover is represented by the links (lines). Read the legend below to see how information about nodes and links are colored. The size of the nodes are also dependent on the amount of copies sold. Hovering over each node reveals more information about the franchise, such as the name, copies sold, genre, and number of crossovers. Clicking on a franchise highlights other games that the current franchise has collaborated with. Finally, you can use the menus to sort the games in order of Copies Sold, # of Crossovers, and Alphabetically. Clicking on any games in the Order menu or the Crossover List will also find the node in the graph.
